About

  • My name is Abdulalim – product designer based in Nigeria. I enjoy creating meaningful and delightful user-centred digital products that balance user needs and business goals. Currently freelancing in Lagos.

    I’m a product designer from Nigeria currently working at Oriad Digitals, Lagos. Before this, I worked with Rovedana, learning and solving complex design problems around human needs.

    Through my design journey, I’ve had the opportunity to relate in both startup and enterprise environments on projects related to literature, healthcare and ecommerce businesses.

    Coming from a background in both agriculture and design, I love connecting the dots between trading, business goals, technical constraints and user satisfaction. I’m passionate about system design and social and environmental causes. In my free time, I enjoy reading, exploring new cuisines, and looking for last minute adventures.
